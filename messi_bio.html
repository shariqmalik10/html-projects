Career highlights
  • The greatest player in football history was born on 24th June, 1987
  • His granddmother was his greatest inspiration and a pillar of support for his love of football
  • According to Leo, she used to take him to all his youth football matches and pushed coaches of his youth football team to put him into the starting squad despite Leo being relatvely younger
  • Leo was also diagnosed with a growth disorder syndrome which resulted in his heigh being a lot shorter than most other players, however through his resilience he kept playing and put incredible shifts at his matches and made his height his weapon.
  • Soon enough his style of play gained traction across Argentina and then Barca's famous 'La Masia' Academy moved to bring in this talented 11 year old who soon alongside teammates like Andres Iniesta, Xavi, Busquets would form the golden generation Barca team under coach Pep Guardiola
